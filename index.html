About Me

I am currently a research intern at the Computer Vision and Multimedia Lab of JD AI Research working with Dr. Wu Liu and Dr. Tao Mei. Meanwhile, I am working toward a Ph.D. degree at the Intelligent Information Processing Laboratory (IIPL), Hangzhou Dianzi University, Zhejiang, China. My supervisors are prof. Jiyong Zhang and Prof. Chenggang Yan. My research interests include gait recognition, vehicle/person re-identification, and computer vision.
